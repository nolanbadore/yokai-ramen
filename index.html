the story behind the flavor.

Yōkai ramen was founded in 2019 by Nolan Badore. Nolan would frequently visit the local ramen shop in downtown Providence, RI. After a short vacation for Christmas, Nolan returned to Providence excited for his next bowl only to find that the ramen shop had shut down. For the next few months he traveled all throughout Providence looking for the next best ramen eatery to no avail. And that's where Yōkai began.

more flavors?

At the moment we only offer our original spicy miso with 3 different packaging types. We are constantly working on new recipes. Be on the lookout for Shio ramen soon.

why yōkai?

Yōkai are spirits from traditional Japanese folklore. Yōkai were created in many cases to explain the unknown or unexplainable. In our case, it's difficult to explain how we created such an incredible delicacy that only needs water and takes 5 minutes.

why not nissin?

Why settle for decency? Yōkai strives to be the best in every facet. Flavor, convenience, price, and quality. We are at the top of all of those pillars.
